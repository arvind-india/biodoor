Biometric Door

A door that will unlock if it recognizes your face.

  • Try App

How does it works?

The door will first study your face and know that you are owner of the house. Next time you are in front of it, the door will remember you and let you in

Input Your Name

Type your name, then go to Step 2. Once you click Snap, your name will be stored in the database.

Go to Webcam

Press button below to trigger webcam. The computer will take 10 shots of your face within 1 second.

Try the Door

Now stand in front of the door and put your face in front of camera. Press Let Me In once you are ready to trigger face recognition.

Wanna Learn More?

Website is built using Flask and Pyrebase. Door is controlled by a Raspberri Pi. Face recognition is done using OpenCV.
